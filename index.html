  1. Shape Outside
    • Wrap text around a general shape including circles, ellipses, rectangles, polygons, custom arbitrary paths. If you inspect with Firefox you can visual tweak the shapes.
    • If an image with transparency is provided, wrap using the transparency edge
  2. Text Background Image
  3. Blend Modes
    • Use the color picker and dropdown menu to play around
    • The color picker is determined by your browser and operating system, so it might look different depending on how you play with the demo
  4. Columns
    • Columns have gaps and optional rules
    • Column rules have styles such as solid, double, dotted and dashed, although they can't be customized too far (yet)
    • Although not technically always a column, lists are often used that way and it is possible to sort from either left to right and then top to bottom, or top to bottom and then left to right.
